# Wizard Overview

Better Robots.txt uses a preset selection screen followed by a structured settings flow.

## Flow structure
- Step 0 — Preset selection
- Step 1 — Search Engine Visibility
- Step 2 — AI & LLM Governance
- Step 3 — SEO Tool Protection
- Step 4 — Bad Bots Protection
- Step 5 — Archive & Wayback Control
- Step 6 — Global Settings
- Step 7 — Spam, Feeds & Crawl Traps
- Step 8 — E-commerce Optimization
- Step 9 — Resources & Assets
- Step 10 — Social Media Crawlers
- Step 11 — Ads & Revenue
- Step 12 — LLMS.txt File
- Step 13 — Advanced Settings
- Step 14 — Review & Save
