# Troubleshooting

## My robots.txt does not look like I expected
Open the final preview step and verify the selected preset and edition first.

## I see features that are locked
Some interface elements belong to Pro or Premium.

## I am unsure which policy fits my site
Start with [Presets](/presets) and [robots.txt Examples](/robots-txt-examples).
