# Presets

Presets help users start from a policy, not from a blank robots.txt file.

## Essential

**Best for:** most WordPress sites, small business sites, and users who want a safe start.

**Why choose it:** it improves the default setup without pushing the site into a restrictive policy too early.

**Not ideal for:** users who already know they need deeper AI, archive, or protection-first controls.

## AI-First

**Best for:** publishers, blogs, editorial teams, and content sites that want to express clearer AI usage preferences.

**Why choose it:** it introduces deeper AI and LLM governance without turning the site into a hard lockdown.

**Not ideal for:** users who simply need a basic safer default and do not want paid controls.

## Fortress

**Best for:** protection-first sites, stricter publishing teams, and websites that want stronger controls around archive, bots, and scraping.

**Why choose it:** it is the strictest guided option in the product.

**Not ideal for:** beginners who just need a clean default configuration.

## Custom

**Best for:** advanced users, agencies, and people who want to build the policy module by module.

**Why choose it:** it gives more freedom when you already understand the trade-offs.

## Which one should most people start with?

For most WordPress websites, **Essential** is the safest place to start.
