# Presets

Presets help you start from a **policy model** instead of a blank robots.txt.

For many WordPress websites, the preset is the smartest place to begin. You can refine later.

> **For most websites, start with Essential.**

## Essential

**Best for**
- most WordPress sites
- small business websites
- site owners who want a safe and practical first setup

**Why choose it**
- It improves the default setup without pushing the site into a restrictive policy too early.
- It is the easiest place to start if you are not technical.
- It gives you a guided path before you decide whether stricter controls are even necessary.

**Not ideal for**
- sites that already know they need deeper AI or archive controls
- users who have a clear reason to pursue a stricter protection-first stance

## AI-First

**Best for**
- publishers
- blogs and editorial teams
- content sites that want to express clearer AI usage preferences

**Why choose it**
- It introduces deeper AI and LLM governance without turning the site into a hard lockdown.
- It is the right fit when you want your policy to distinguish more clearly between search, answer generation, and training.

**Not ideal for**
- people who only need a basic safer default
- people who do not need paid controls at all

## Fortress

**Best for**
- protection-first sites
- stricter publishing teams
- websites that want stronger controls around archive, bots, and scraping

**Why choose it**
- It is the strictest guided option in the product.
- It is useful when openness is not your first priority.
- It fits cases where archive capture, scraping, or broader bot exposure is a real concern.

**Not ideal for**
- beginners who simply need a clean default configuration
- sites that benefit from a more open discovery model

## Custom

**Best for**
- advanced users
- agencies
- people who want to build the policy module by module

**Why choose it**
- It gives more freedom when you already understand the trade-offs.
- It is the right option when your site does not fit neatly into a standard preset profile.

**Not ideal for**
- users who are still learning how different crawler categories affect their site
- people who want the fastest safe starting point

## Presets by business type

| Business type | Recommended preset |
| --- | --- |
| Small business website | Essential |
| Content publisher | AI-First |
| WooCommerce store | Essential or AI-First |
| Protection-first or archive-sensitive site | Fortress |
| Agency / advanced user | Custom |

## Common mistakes

- Choosing Fortress too early without understanding the trade-offs
- Expecting a preset to act like a firewall
- Overblocking social or useful discovery bots without a clear reason
- Starting with Custom before you understand the product logic
