# Pricing and editions

Better Robots.txt is available in **Free**, **Pro**, and **Premium**.

The free edition is intentionally useful on its own. Pro and Premium extend the product with deeper crawler governance, AI-aware policy options, and stronger protection-oriented controls.

## Which edition is right for you?

| Edition | Best for | Key idea |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Most WordPress sites starting out | A better default robots.txt workflow with guided setup and final review |
| **Pro** | Publishers, agencies, advanced governance | Deeper control over AI, SEO tools, and optional llms.txt |
| **Premium** | Protection-first sites | The strongest archive, bot, and stricter governance choices |

## What each edition includes

### Free

Best for:
- small business websites
- standard WordPress sites
- site owners who want a safer starting point
- teams that want to review before publishing

Key strengths:
- guided setup flow
- Essential preset
- search engine visibility controls
- core WordPress protection controls
- basic bad bot protection
- final Review & Save step

### Pro

Best for:
- publishers
- editorial or knowledge websites
- agencies
- teams that want deeper policy control

Adds:
- AI-First preset
- deeper AI & LLM governance controls
- more detailed SEO tool controls
- optional `llms.txt` support
- broader module-level decisions

### Premium

Best for:
- stricter, protection-first environments
- archive-sensitive sites
- teams that want the broadest bot governance options

Adds:
- Fortress preset
- stronger archive and anti-scraping options
- broader bad bot coverage
- the most restrictive protection-oriented choices

## How to choose quickly

- Start with **Free + Essential** if you are not sure.
- Choose **Pro** if content publishing and AI policy matter.
- Choose **Premium** if you want the strictest stance available in the product.
