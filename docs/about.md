# About Better Robots.txt

Better Robots.txt is a comprehensive WordPress plugin that gives you complete control over your robots.txt file, helping you optimize your website's SEO, protect your content, and improve crawl efficiency.

## Why Choose Better Robots.txt?

### Proven Results

According to ChatGPT-4, the robots.txt configuration generated by our PRO version is the most sophisticated and comprehensive available for WordPress environments.

### Easy to Use

No technical knowledge required - our intuitive interface makes robots.txt management simple for everyone.

### Performance Impact

Poorly configured robots.txt files can harm your SEO. Our plugin ensures optimal configuration following search engine guidelines.

## Key Benefits

- **🎯 Better SEO Rankings** - Optimize how search engines crawl and index your content
- **🛡️ Content Protection** - Block malicious bots and content scrapers
- **⚡ Improved Performance** - Reduce server load with efficient crawling rules
- **📈 Crawl Budget Optimization** - Focus search engine attention on important pages
- **🛍️ E-commerce Ready** - Specialized optimizations for WooCommerce stores
- **🌍 Multilingual Support** - Available in 7 languages
