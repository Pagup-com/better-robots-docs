# AI & LLM Governance

**Location:** `Step 2 - AI & LLM Governance`

This step controls how AI systems are handled in your site's robots rules.

## Main Controls

- **AI Training Protection**
  Blocks the training-bot group when enabled
- **AI Search & Answer Engines**
  Chooses between:
  - `Block All AI Search`
  - `Allow AI Search Engines`

The separate `/llms.txt` file is documented in [LLMS.txt File](/settings/llms-txt).
