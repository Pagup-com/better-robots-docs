# AI & LLM Governance

**Location:** Step 2 — AI & LLM Governance

This step controls how AI-related crawlers and usage preferences are handled.

## Main controls

- **AI Training Protection** — blocks training-oriented bots when enabled
- **AI Search & Answer Engines** — lets you choose whether AI search systems should be blocked or allowed
- **Content usage signals** — clarifies preferences such as search, ai-input, and ai-train
- **llms.txt** — available separately when your edition supports it

## Why this step matters

This is where Better Robots.txt becomes more than a simple robots.txt editor. It lets you express intent around AI access and usage while staying clear that these are declarative signals.

## Editions

- Free: basic AI-related control
- Pro: deeper AI governance and llms.txt
- Premium: strongest governance-oriented setup
